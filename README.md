# VidTextBot

VidTextBot is an AI-powered application that converts video content into meaningful text summaries and enables users to interact with the extracted content through a chatbot interface. The system automatically processes video input, extracts audio, converts speech to text, and generates concise summaries, making video information easier to understand, search, and reuse.

The chatbot allows users to ask questions related to the video content and receive accurate, context-aware responses, helping users quickly grasp key ideas without watching the entire video.

Key Features

Audio extraction from video files
Speech-to-text transcription
Automatic text summarization
Chatbot for interactive Q&A on video content
Faster understanding of long videos


Use Cases

Educational video summarization
Lecture and tutorial analysis
Meeting or webinar content extraction
Quick review of long video materials

VidTextBot simplifies video content consumption by transforming videos into interactive, text-based knowledge.
